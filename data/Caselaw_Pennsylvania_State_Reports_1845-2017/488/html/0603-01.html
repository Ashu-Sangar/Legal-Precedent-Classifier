OPINION

PER CURIAM.

The lower court has not yet acted on appellees’ adoption petition. Thus, the Order appealed from is not final and not properly subject to review at this time. See Adoption of G. M., 484 Pa. 24, 398 A.2d 642 (1979).

Accordingly, the appeal is quashed, appellants to pay costs.
