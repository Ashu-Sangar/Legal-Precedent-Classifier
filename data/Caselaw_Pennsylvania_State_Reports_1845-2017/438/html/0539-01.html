Opinion

Per Curiam,

The order of the Superior Court is reversed and the case remanded to the hearing court for the filing of a proper order in conformity with the Act of January 25, 1966, P. L. (1965) 1580, §10, 19 P.S. §1180-10 (Supp. 1969), which requires that “[t]he order finally disposing of the petition shall state grounds on which the case was determined and whether a federal or a state right was presented and decided.” See Commonwealth v. Naylor, 437 Pa. 193, 262 A. 2d 146 (1970).
