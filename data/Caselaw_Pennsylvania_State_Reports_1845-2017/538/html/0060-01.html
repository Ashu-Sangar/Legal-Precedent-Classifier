645 A.2d 1287

In the Matter of John J. McGEE. Petition For Reinstatement

No. 679, Disciplinary Docket No. 2.

Disciplinary Board No. 108 DB 89.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Aug. 3, 1994.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 3rd day of August, 1994, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board of the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania dated July 7, 1994, the Petition for Reinstatement is granted.

Pursuant to Rule 218(e), Pa.R.D.E., petitioner is directed to pay the expenses incurred by the Board in the investigation and processing of the Petition for Reinstatement.

MONTEMURO, J., is sitting by designation as Senior Justice pursuant to Judicial Assignment Docket No. 94 R1801, due to the unavailability of LARSEN, J., see No. 127 Judicial Administration Docket No. 1, filed October 28, 1993.
