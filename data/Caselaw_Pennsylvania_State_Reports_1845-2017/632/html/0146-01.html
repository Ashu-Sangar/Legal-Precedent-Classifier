118 A.3d 385

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Petitioner v. Michael STEVENS, Respondent.

No. 43 EM 2015.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

July 2, 2015.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 2nd day of July, 2015, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is DISMISSED. Only final orders of the *147Superior Court may be challenged through allocatur review. See 42 Pa.C.S. § 724(a). The Superior Court’s remand order, which specifically noted that jurisdiction was retained, was not final. See Pa.R.A.P. 1112(b) (defining a final order, for purposes of allocatur, as one which “concludes an appeal, including an order that remands an appeal, ... unless the appellate court remands and retains jurisdiction”).
