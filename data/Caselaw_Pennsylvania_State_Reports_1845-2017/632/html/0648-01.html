122 A.3d 1033

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent v. Teron LEWIS, Petitioner.

No. 102 MM 2015.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 16, 2015.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 16th day of September, 2015, the Petition for Leave to File Petition for Allowance of Appeal Nunc Pro Tunc is DENIED, WITHOUT PREJUDICE to Petitioner’s right to seek similar relief through the Post Conviction Relief Act. See 42 Pa.C.S. § 9545(b) (explaining that, generally, a Post Conviction Relief Act petition shall be filed within one year of the date of when the judgment of sentence becomes final).
