AND NOW, this 21st day of July, 2010, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, rephrased for clarity, is

Whether the Superior Court erred in holding that it is per se reversible error to seat a discharged alternate juror after jury deliberations have begun.
