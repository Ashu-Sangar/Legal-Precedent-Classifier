47 A.3d 1173

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent v. Gordon Charles WILLIAMS, Petitioner.

No. 601 MAL 2011.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 13, 2012.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 13th day of June, 2012, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, LIMITED TO the issue set forth below. Allocatur is DENIED as to all remaining issues. The issue, as stated by Petitioner, is:

Whether a Defendant has a right to present informed expert testimony to rebut the Commonwealth’s evidence in support of its motion pursuant to 42 Pa.C.S.A. § 5985 to allow a child witness to testify in a room separate from courtroom proceedings?
