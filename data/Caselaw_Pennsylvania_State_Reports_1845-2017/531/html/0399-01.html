ORDER

PER CURIAM.

The Order of the Commonwealth Court quashing the appeal and vacating the Order of the Court of Common Pleas of Allegheny County is reversed. See Appeal of Borough of Churchill, 525 Pa. 80, 575 A.2d 550 (1990). The appeal is reinstated and the case is remanded to the Commonwealth Court for a decision on the merits.

PAPADAKOS, J., did not participate in the decision of this case.
