655 A.2d 502

In the Matter of George E. GOLDSTEIN.

No. 394, Disciplinary Docket No. 2.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 7, 1995.

PETITION FOR REINSTATEMENT

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 7th day of February, 1995, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board of the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania dated December 28, 1994, the Petition for Reinstatement is granted.

Pursuant to Rule 218(e), Pa.R.D.E., petitioner is directed to pay the expenses incurred by the Board in the investigation and processing of the Petition for Reinstatement.

MONTEMURO, J., is sitting by designation.
