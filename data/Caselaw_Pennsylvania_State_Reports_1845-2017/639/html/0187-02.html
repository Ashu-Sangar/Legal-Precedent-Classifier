PER CURIAM

AND NOW, this 29th day of December, 2016, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, rephrased for clarity, is:

In uninsured motorist claims subject to mandatory arbitration, is the statute of limitations tolled only by the commencement of an official judicial action, or may extrajudicial actions also toll the statute of limitations?
