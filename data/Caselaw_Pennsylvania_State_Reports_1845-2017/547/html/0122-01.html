688 A.2d 1178

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Stephen J. MARCINCIN, Respondent.

No. 301 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 6, 1997.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 6th day of February, 1997, there having been filed with this Court by Stephen J. Marcincin his verified Statement of Resignation dated December 13, 1996, stating that he desires to resign from the Bar of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania in accordance with the provisions of Rule 215, Pa.R.D.E., it is

ORDERED that the resignation of Stephen J. Marcincin be and it is hereby accepted and he is DISBARRED ON CONSENT from the Bar of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania; and it is further ORDERED that he shall comply with the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E. Respondent shall pay costs, *123if any, to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.
