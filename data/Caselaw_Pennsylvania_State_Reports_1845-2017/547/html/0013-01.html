688 A.2d 689

In the Matter of Edward K. STRAUSS.

No. 632, Disciplinary Docket No. 2.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Dec. 30, 1996.

PETITION FOR REINSTATEMENT

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 30th day of December, 1996, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board of the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania dated November 6, 1996, the Petition for Reinstatement is granted.

Pursuant to Rule 218(e), Pa.R.D.E., petitioner is directed to pay the expenses incurred by the Board in the investigation and processing of the Petition for Reinstatement.

ZAPPALA, J., did not participate in this matter.
