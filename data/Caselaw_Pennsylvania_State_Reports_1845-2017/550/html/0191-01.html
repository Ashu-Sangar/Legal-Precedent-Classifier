704 A.2d 612

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. James L. HEIDECKER, Jr., Respondent.

No. 345 Disciplinary No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Dec. 19, 1997.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 19th day of December, 1997, on certification by the Disciplinary Board that the respondent, JAMES L. HEIDECKER, JR., who was suspended by Order of this Court dated July 29, 1997, for a period of three months, has filed a verified statement showing compliance with all the terms and conditions of the Order of Suspension and Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E., and there being no other outstanding order of suspension or disbarment, JAMES L. HEIDECKER, JR. is hereby reinstated to active status, effective immediately.
