703 A.2d 706

In the Matter of Joseph T. MARGRABIA, Jr.

No. 201 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Dec. 16, 1997.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 16th day of December, 1997, Joseph T. Margrabia, Jr., having been suspended from the practice of law in the State of New Jersey for a period of three months by Order of the Supreme Court of New Jersey dated July 11, 1997; the said Joseph T. Margrabia, Jr., having been directed on October 9, 1997, to inform this Court of any claim he has that the imposition of the identical or comparable discipline in this Commonwealth would be unwarranted and the reasons therefor; and no response having been filed, it is

ORDERED that Joseph T. Margrabia, Jr., is suspended from the practice of law in this Commonwealth for a period of three months, and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.
