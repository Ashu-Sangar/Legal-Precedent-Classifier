708 A.2d 490

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Yale F. EDEIKEN, Respondent.

No. 401 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

April 20, 1998.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 20th day of April, 1998, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board dated February 26,1998, it is hereby

ORDERED that YALE F. EDEIKEN be subjected to PUBLIC CENSURE by the Supreme Court.

It is further ORDERED that respondent shall pay costs to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.
