*347Argued March 21, 1951.

Before Drew, C. J., Stern, Stearne, Jones, Bell, Ladner and Chidsey, JJ.

*350 Samuel J. Feigus, with him Wade K. Newell, for appellant.

William B. Parshall, with him Henderson, Parshall & Crow, for appellee.

May 23,1951:

Opinion

Per Curiam,

A majority of the members of this Court who heard this case are of the opinion that the learned court below was correct in refusing to take off the nonsuit. The *351judgment is therefore affirmed on the opinion of President Judge Care for the court en banc.

Judgment affirmed.
