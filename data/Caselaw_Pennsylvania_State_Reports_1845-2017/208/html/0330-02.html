Glaser, Appellant, v. Glenwood Railroad Company.

(No. 3).

Argued Nov. 3, 1903.

Reargued Feb. 2, 1904.

Appeal, No. 44, Oct. T., 1903, by plaintiff, from decree of C. P. No. 3, Allegheny Co., Nov. T., 1902, No. 465, dismissing bill in equity in case of Rosanna Glaser, John K. Glaser, Adam T. Glaser, James G. Glaser, Charles A. Glaser and Lizzie B. Glaser v. Glenwood Railroad Company.

Before Mitchell, O. J., Dean, Fell, Brown, Mestrezat, Potter and Thompson, JJ.

Affirmed.

March 7, 1904:

Opinion by

Mr. Justice Dean,

This case is ruled by Dryden v. Railroad Company, ante, p. 316. For the reasons given in that opinion, the decree is affirmed and the appeal is dismissed at costs of appellants.
