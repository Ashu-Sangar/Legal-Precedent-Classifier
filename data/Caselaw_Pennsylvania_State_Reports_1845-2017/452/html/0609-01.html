Dibofsky v. Young et al., Appellants.

Argued January 11, 1973.

Before Jones, C. J., Eagen, O’Brien, Egberts, Pomeroy, Nix and ManDERINO, JJ.

*610 Q. Wayne Renneisen, with him Bruce D. Lombardo, and Harvey, Pennington, Herting and Renneisen, for appellant.

Paul J. Downey, with him Joseph Alessandroni, Jr., for appellee.

May 4, 1973:

Opinion

Per Curiam,

The order of the Superior Court affirming the order of the Court of Common Pleas of Philadelphia, Civil Division, overruling defendants’ preliminary objections is hereby reversed on the authority of Yefko v. Ochs, 437 Pa. 233, 263 A. 2d 416 (1970), and the matter is remanded for proceedings consistent with this disposition.

Mr. Justice Roberts and Mr. Justice Manderino dissent.
