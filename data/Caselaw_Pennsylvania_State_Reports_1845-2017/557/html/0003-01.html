731 A.2d 128

WAREHIME ENTERPRISES, INC., Petitioner, v. John WAREHIME and Patricia Warehime, Husband and Wife, Respondents.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 9, 1999.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 9th day of June, 1999, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED, limited to the following two issues:

Did the Superior Court err in concluding that the Petitioner failed to state a cause of action against Respondent John Warehime for breach of fiduciary duty and/or breach of the duty of loyalty and good faith owed to the Petitioner by each of its directors?
Whether 15 Pa.C.S. § 1728 defines the outer limits of a director’s fiduciary duties of care, good faith and/or loyalty in connection with an interested transaction which the director knows will result in unfairness or fraud to the corporation?
