Marshall J. Piccinini, Erie, Robert A. Graci, Harrisburg, for Com.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

Based upon our disposition in Commonwealth v. Williams, 557 Pa. 285, 733 A.2d 593 (1999), we affirm the Order of the Erie County Court of Common Pleas dated September 17, 1998. Furthermore, we remand the matter to the common pleas court for disposition of any remaining issues.

Jurisdiction is relinquished.
