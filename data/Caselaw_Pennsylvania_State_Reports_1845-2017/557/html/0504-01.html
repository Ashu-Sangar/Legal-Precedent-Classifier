PER CURIAM.

Based upon our disposition in Commonwealth v. Williams, 557 Pa. 285, 733 A.2d 593 (1999), we affirm the Order of the Crawford County Court of Common Pleas dated June 2, 1998. Furthermore,' we remand the matter to the common pleas court for disposition of any remaining issues.

Jurisdiction is relinquished.
