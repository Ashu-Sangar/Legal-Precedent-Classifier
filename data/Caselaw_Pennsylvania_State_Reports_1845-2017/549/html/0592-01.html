702 A.2d 362

Albert N. FLEMING and Johnny B. Fleming, Petitioner & Cross-Respondent, v. CHEVRON U.S.A., INC., Respondent & Cross-Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Nov. 26, 1997.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 26th day of November, 1997, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED and the Cross-Petition for Allowance of Appeal is DENIED. The decision of the Superior Court is REVERSED and the case is REMANDED to the Court of Common Pleas of Philadelphia County for further proceedings. See Cheeseman v. Lethal Exterminator, Inc., 549 Pa. 200, 701 A.2d 156 (1997).

Jurisdiction is relinquished.
