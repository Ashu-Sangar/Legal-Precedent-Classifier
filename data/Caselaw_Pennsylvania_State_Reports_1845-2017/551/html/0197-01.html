ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW this 20th day of April, 1998, the petition for allowance of appeal is granted and the order of the Commonwealth Court is reversed. See Udvari v. Workmen’s Compensation Appeal Board (USAir, Inc.), 705 A.2d 1290 (Pa. 1997). The Request for Supersedeas is denied as moot.
