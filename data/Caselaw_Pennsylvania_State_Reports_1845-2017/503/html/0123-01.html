McDERMOTT, Justice,

Offering the approach which I proposed in my concurring opinion in Commonwealth v. Crowley, 502 Pa. 393, 466 A.2d 1009 (1983), I dissent from this court’s per curiam affirmance of the Superior Court’s order, reversing the trial court’s judgment of sentence.
