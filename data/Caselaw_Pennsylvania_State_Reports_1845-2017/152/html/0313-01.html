McMasters v. Feltyberger, Appellant.

Appeal, No. 166, Oct. T., 1892, by defendant, Samuel Feltyberger, from judgment of C. P. No. 3, Allegheny Co., Aug. T., 1891, No. 360, on verdict for plaintiff, David M. McMasters. Argued with preceding case.

J. J. Miller and B. T. Watson, for appellant.

A. M. Brown, It. B. Stewart with him, for appellee.

January 3, 1893:

Opinion by

Mr. Chiee Justice Paxson,

This case is ruled by McMasters v. Negley, decided herewith. Any further discussion of the question involved is unnecessary.

Judgment reversed.

Mr. Justice Sterrett took no part in the decisions of these cases.
