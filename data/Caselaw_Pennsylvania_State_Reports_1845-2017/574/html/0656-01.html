ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 29th day of September 2003, we GRANT the Petition for Allowance of Appeal, and LIMIT it to the following question:

To what extent can the courts limit parents from advocating religious beliefs that, if acted upon, would constitute criminal conduct?
