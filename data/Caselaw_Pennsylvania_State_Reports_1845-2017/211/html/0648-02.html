Stamey, Appellant, v. McCreery.

Argued Oct. 18, 1904.

Appeal, No. 37, Oct. T., 1904, by plaintiffs, from judgment of C. P. Indiana Co., June T., 1903, No. 159, on verdict for defendants in issue framed between W. H. Stamey, J. C. King and E. D. Carter as plaintiffs and A. McCreery as defendant.

Before Mitchell, C. J., Dean, Fell, Brown, Mestrezat, Potter and Elkin, JJ.

Affirmed.

April 10, 1905:

Opinion by

Mr. Justice Brown,

The questions raised by the assignments of error in this case *649are the same as those raised in Stamey et al. v. Barkley, 211 Pa. 313, in which we have this day filed an opinion affirming the judgment below, and, for the reasons therein given, this judgment is affirmed.
