Stamey, Appellant, v. Templeton.

Argued Oct. 18, 1904.

Appeal, No. 42, Oct. T., 1904, by plaintiffs, from judgment of C. P. Indiana Co., June T., 1903, No 165, on verdict for defendant iii issue framed between W. H. Stamey, J. C. King and E. D. Carter as plaintiffs and A. S. Templeton as defendant.

Before Mitchell, C. J., Dean, Fell, Bbown, Mestbezat, Potteb and Elkin, JJ.

Affirmed.

April 10, 1905 :

Opinion by

Mb. Justice Bbown,

The questions raised by the assignments of error in this case are the same as those raised in Stamey et al. v. Barkley, 211 Pa. 313, in which we have this day filed an opinion affirming the judgment below, and, for the reasons therein given, this judgment is affirmed.
