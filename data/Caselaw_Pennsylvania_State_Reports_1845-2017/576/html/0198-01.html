839 A.2d 167

In the Matter of Ara Richard AVRIGIAN.

No. 842 DISC. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 22, 2003.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 22nd day of September, 2003, Ara Richard Avrigian having been suspended from the practice of law in the State of New Jersey for a period of three months by Order of the Supreme Court of New Jersey dated February 21, 2003; the said Ara Richard Avrigian having been directed on June 11, 2003, to inform this Court of any claim he has that the imposition of the identical or comparable discipline in this Commonwealth would be unwarranted and the reasons therefor; and upon consideration of the responses filed, it is

ORDERED that Ara Richard Avrigian is suspended from the practice of law in this Commonwealth for a period of three *199months, and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.
