839 A.2d 178

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Jay MARTILLOTTI, Respondent.

No. 118 DB 2003.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Nov. 18, 2003.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 18th day of November, 2008, upon consideration of the Recommendation of the Disciplinary Board dated October 2, 2003, it is hereby

ORDERED that Jay Martillotti is placed on temporary suspension pursuant to Rule 208(f), Pa.R.D.E., until further definitive action by this Court. It is further ORDERED that respondent shall comply with the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.

This Order constitutes an imposition of public discipline within the meaning of Rule 402, Pa.R.D.E., pertaining to confidentiality.
