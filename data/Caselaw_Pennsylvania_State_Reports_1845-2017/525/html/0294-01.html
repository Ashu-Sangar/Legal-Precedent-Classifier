580 A.2d 294

Eddie GREEN and Bobbi Green, Petitioners, v. Roger JOHNSON and Diane Johnson, Respondents.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Aug. 7, 1990.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 7th day of August, 1990, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is granted. The Order of the Superior Court quashing the appeal is vacated and the matter is remanded to that court for disposition of the appeal on the merits.

Application of Pa.R.A.P. 311(a)(1), as amended March 31, 1989, effective July 1, 1989, to this case is not “just and practicable,” especially in view of the time and money expended by the petitioners in filing their appeal before the *295new Rule was published, and in briefing and arguing the appeal before the effective date of the new Rule.
