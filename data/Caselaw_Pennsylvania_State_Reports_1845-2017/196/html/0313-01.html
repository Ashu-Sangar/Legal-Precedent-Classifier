O’Neill v. Philadelphia.

Argued Jan. 15, 1900.

Appeal, No. 308, Jan. T., 1899, by plaintiff, from order of C. P. No. 3, Phila. Co., Sept. T.,1897, No. 333, sustaining exceptions to report of referee in case of •James O’Neill v. Philadelphia.

Before Green, C. J., Mitchell, Dean, Fell, Brown and Mestrezat, JJ.

Affirmed.

May 29, 1900:

Opinion by

Mr. Justice Brown,

This appeal raises the same question that was presented to us in Betham v. The City of Philadelphia, January term, 1899, No. 307, just decided, and, for the reasons therein given, the Judgment of the court below is affirmed.

Judgment affirmed.
