No. 1082 DISC. 3.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 1st day of December, 2005, the Joint Petition for Temporary Suspension is granted and, pursuant to Rule 214, Pa.R.D.E., Laurie Jill Besden is placed on temporary suspension and she shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E..
