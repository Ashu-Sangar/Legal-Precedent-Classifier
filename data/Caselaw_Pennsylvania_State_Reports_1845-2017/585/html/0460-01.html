888 A.2d 815

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent v. Shahram NAHAVANDIAN, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Jan. 4, 2006.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 4th day of January, 2006, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED, limited to the following issue:

Was the Commonwealth’s evidence sufficient to support Petitioner’s conviction for Drug Delivery Resulting in Death, 18 Pa.C.S. § 2506?

The order of the Superior Court is hereby VACATED, and the matter is REMANDED for consideration in light of Commonwealth v. Ludwig, 583 Pa. 6, 874 A.2d 623 (2005).

Jurisdiction relinquished.
