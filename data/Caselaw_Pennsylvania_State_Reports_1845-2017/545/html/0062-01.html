679 A.2d 1262

Linda Parisano DALRYMPLE, Petitioner, v. Eugene BROWN, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

July 24, 1996.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 24th day of July, 1996, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED, limited to the issue of whether psychological repression of a traumatic event, which is allegedly only recalled after the statute of limitations has run, should be recognized in Pennsylvania.
