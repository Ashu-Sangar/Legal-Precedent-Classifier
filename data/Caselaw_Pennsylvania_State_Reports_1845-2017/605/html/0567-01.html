992 A.2d 845

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Petitioner v. Jason HOLMES, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

April 8, 2010.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 8th day of April, 2010, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED as to the following issue:

Whether the Superior Court erred in reversing the Defendant’s judgment of sentence, based on a misapplication of the relevant precedent permitting stops of motor vehicles for an alleged violation of 75 Pa.C.S. § 4524(c) (windshield obstructions and wipers)?
