ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 28th day of January, 2010, this appeal from the Commonwealth Court’s September 17, 2009 Order denying Appellants Diploma Joint Insurance Fund’s and Neptune Board of Education’s Petition to Intervene is quashed. The Commonwealth Court’s Order does not satisfy the requirements of an immediately appealable collateral order under Pa.R.A.P. 313.
