54 A.3d 13

In re Petition for EMPANELMENT OF INDICTING GRAND JURY. Petition of First Judicial District.

No. 138 EM 2012.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 27, 2012.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 27th day of September, 2012, the “Petition for Empanelment of Indicting Grand Jury” is GRANTED. The First Judicial District has the authority to empanel an indicting grand jury on or after December 18, 2012 in accordance with Pa.R.Crim.P. 556 et seq.
