ORDER

PER CURIAM.

The petition for allowance of appeal is granted and the order of Superior Court, 334 Pa.Super. 53, 482 A.2d 1076, is reversed. See Matter of G.T.M., 506 Pa. 44, 483 A.2d 1355 (1984). The final decree of Erie County Court of Common *79Pleas, Orphans’ Court Division, terminating the parental rights of the natural mother of A.F., is reinstated.
