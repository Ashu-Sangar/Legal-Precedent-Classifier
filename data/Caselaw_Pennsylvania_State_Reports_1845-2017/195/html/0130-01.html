Margaret J. and Hannah E. Marsh, Executrices of James Marsh, Deceased, v. Philadelphia, Appellant.

Argued Jan. 26, 1900.

Appeal, No. 259, Jan. T., 1899, by defendant, from judgment of C. P. No. 4, Phila. Co., Sept. T., 1894, No. 876, on verdict for plaintiff.

Before McCollum;, Mitchell, Fell, Brows and Mesteezat, JJ,

Reversed.

March 12, 1900 :

Opinion by

Me. Justice Mitchell,

This case is exactly similar in its circumstances to Stork v. Philadelphia, ante, p. 101, opinion filed herewith, and the judgment is reversed for the reasons there given.
