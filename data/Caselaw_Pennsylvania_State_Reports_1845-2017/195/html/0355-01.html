Opinion by

Mr. Justice Mestrezat,

Fot' tbe reasons given in tbe opinion this day filed in the case of Robert W. Finletter, etc., v. Katharine P. Appleton et al. at No. 374, January term, 1899, the assignment of error in this case is overruled, and the decree is affirmed.
