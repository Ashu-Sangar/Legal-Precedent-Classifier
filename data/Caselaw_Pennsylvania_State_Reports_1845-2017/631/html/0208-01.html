ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 27th day of January, 2015, the Petition for Leave to File Petition for Allowance of Appeal Nunc Pro Tunc is GRANTED. Counsel is DIRECTED to file a Petition for Allowance of Appeal within 15 days of this order.
