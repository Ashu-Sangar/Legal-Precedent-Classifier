Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 14th day of August, 1998, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is granted limited to the question:

“When an otherwise illegal search is sought to be validated on the basis of purported consent, must the court employ a waiver analysis, as opposed to a simple ‘voluntariness’ standard.”
