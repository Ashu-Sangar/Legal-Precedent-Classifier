715 A.2d 427

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Donald B. HOYT, Respondent.

No. 438 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Aug. 5, 1998.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 5th day of August, 1998, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board dated June 4,1998, it is hereby

ORDERED that DONALD B. HOYT be subjected to PUBLIC CENSURE by the Supreme Court.

It is further ORDERED that respondent shall pay costs to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.
