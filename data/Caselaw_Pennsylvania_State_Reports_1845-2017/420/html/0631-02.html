Opinion

Per Curiam,

This is an appeal from an order below' dismissing' an action in habeas corpus without hearing.

We affirm. The following recent decisions are controlling: Commonwealth ex rel. Blackshear v. Myers, 419 Pa. 151, 213 A. 2d 378 (1965); Commonwealth ex rel. Knowles v. Rundle, 419 Pa. 300, 213 A. 2d 635 (1965); and, Commonwealth ex rel. Adderley v. Myers, 418 Pa. 366, 211 A. 2d 481 (1965).

Order affirmed.
