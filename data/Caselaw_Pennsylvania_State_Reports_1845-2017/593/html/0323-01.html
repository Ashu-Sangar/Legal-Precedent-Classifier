July 20, 2007.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 20th day of July, 2007, the Petition for Review is dismissed for lack of jurisdiction and the Application to Lodge is denied. See 1 Pa.Code § 35.20 (providing that “[a]n action taken by a subordinate officer under authority delegated by the agency head may be appealed to the agency head by filing a petition within 10 days after service of notice of the action.”).
