Commonwealth ex rel. Currier, Appellant, v. Russell.

Submitted October 9, 1964.

Before Bell, C. J., Musmanno, Jones, Cohen, Eagen, O’Brien and Roberts, JJ.

*543 Leonard J. Currier, appellant, in propria persona.

Ralph B. D’lorio, Assistant District Attorney, Dómeme D. Jerome, First Assistant District Attorney, and Jacques H. Fox, District Attorney, for appellee.

November 10, 1964:

Opinion

Per Curiam,

In the light of onr decisions in Commonwealth ex rel. O’Lock v. Rundle, 415 Pa. 515, 204 A. 2d 489 (1964), and Commonwealth ex rel. Goodfellow v. Rundle, 415 Pa. 528, 204 A. 2d 446 (1964), the orders of the Superior Court and the Court of Common Pleas of Delaware County are reversed. The record is remanded to the court of original jurisdiction with directions to issue the writ and grant a new trial.
