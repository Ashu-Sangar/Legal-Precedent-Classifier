758 A.2d 655

In the Matter of Mark David MAZZA.

No. 588 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Aug. 3, 2000.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 3rd day of August, 2000, a Rule having been entered by this Court on May 15, 2000, pursuant to Rule *129214(d)(1), Pa.R.D.E., directing Mark David Mazza to show cause why he should not be placed on temporary suspension and no response thereto having been filed, it is hereby

ORDERED that the Rule is made absolute; Mark David Mazza is placed on temporary suspension and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.; and the matter is referred to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 214(f)(1), Pa.R.D.E.
