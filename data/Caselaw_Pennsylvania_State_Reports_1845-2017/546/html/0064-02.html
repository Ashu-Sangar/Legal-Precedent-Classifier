683 A.2d 253

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Mary Pope PORTIS, Respondent.

No. 248 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Aug. 13, 1996.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 13th day of August, 1996, there having been filed with this Court by Mary Pope Portis her verified *65Statement of Resignation dated June 25,1996, stating that she desires to resign from the Bar of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania in accordance with the provisions of Rule 215, Pa.R.D.E., it is

ORDERED that the resignation of Mary Pope Portis be and it is hereby accepted and she is DISBARRED ON CONSENT from the Bar of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania; and it is further ORDERED that she shall comply with the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E. Respondent shall pay costs, if any, to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.
