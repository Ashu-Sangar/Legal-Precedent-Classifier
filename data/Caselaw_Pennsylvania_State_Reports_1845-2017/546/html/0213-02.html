ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 31st day of October, 1996, we GRANT Petitioner’s Petition for Allowance of Appeal, VACATE Petitioner’s 75 Pa.C.S. § 3731(a)(5) conviction pursuant to this *214court’s decision in Commonwealth v.
