552 A.2d 1036

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania v. Girlie SINGH, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Jan. 18, 1989.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

Petition for Allowance of Appeal is granted, limited to the issue whether trial counsel was ineffective for failing to request a jury instruction that prior acts of violence by the victim could be considered in support of the self-defense claim that deadly force was reasonable.
