Opinion

per curiam,

On this appeal from a decree refusing a preliminary injunction, it appears that there were reasonable grounds for the action of the learned court below. The decree is, therefore, affirmed. See Cohen et al. v. A. M. Byers Company et al., 363 Pa. 618, 70 A. 2d 837.

Decree affirmed at appellant’s costs.
