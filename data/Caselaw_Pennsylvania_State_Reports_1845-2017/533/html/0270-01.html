621 A.2d 563

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Petitioner, v. Albert JONES, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 26, 1993.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

The Petition for Allowance of Appeal is granted. The order of the Superior Court is reversed. We have previously held that Commonwealth v. Thomas, 520 Pa. 206, 553 A.2d 918 (1989) shall be applied prospectively only. The judgment of the Court of Common Pleas of Philadelphia County is reinstated.
