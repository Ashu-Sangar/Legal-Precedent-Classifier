Mager, Appellant, v. Kulp.

Argued January 8, 1965.

Before Bell, O. J., Musmanno, Jones, Cohen, Eagen, O’Brien and Roberts, JJ.

reargument refused April 7, 1965.

March 16, 1965:

Cyril L. Weston, for appellants.

Robert M. Mountenay, with him Smith, Mountenay, Mims & Wilson, for appellees.

Opinion

Per Curiam,

This appeal raises the question of whether a zoning ordinance had been validly enacted. The lower *631Court sustained its validity. We find no merit in any of appellants’ contentions.

Order affirmed.
