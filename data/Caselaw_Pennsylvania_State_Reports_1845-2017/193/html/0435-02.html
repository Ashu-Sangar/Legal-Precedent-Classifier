Argued Oct. 24, 1899.

Appeal, No. 50, Oct. T., 1899, by William Roseberg, from order of C. P. No. 2, Allegheny Co., Jan. T., 1895, No. 361, dismissing exceptions to reportof viewers.

Before Sterrett, C. J., Green, McCollum, Mitchell, Dean, Fell and Brown, JJ.

Affirmed.

October 30, 1899 :

Per. Curiam,

We find no error in this record that requires us to sustam any of the specifications of error. For the reasons given in the opinion of the learned judge of the court below, ante, p. 432, he was clearly right in dismissing appellant’s exceptions.

Decree affirmed and appeal dismissed at appellant’s costs.
