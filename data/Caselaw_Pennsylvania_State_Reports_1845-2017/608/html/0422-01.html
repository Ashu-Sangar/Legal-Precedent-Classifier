PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 12th day of JANUARY, 2011, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, rephrased for clarity, is:

Does the Public Official and Employee Ethics Act, 65 Pa.C.S. § 1101, et seq., expressly grant County Commissioners in Second Class A Counties the right to exercise limited supervisory authority over employees of Row Offices for purposes of enforcing the Ethics Act’s remedial purposes?
