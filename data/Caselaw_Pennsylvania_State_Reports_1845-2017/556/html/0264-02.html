ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 28 th day of April, 1999, we GRANT the Petition for Allowance of Appeal LIMITED to the following issue:

In determining whether the statute of limitations has been tolled, must the court inquire into whether the plaintiff has made good faith efforts to serve process after the initial thirty-day service period has expired?
