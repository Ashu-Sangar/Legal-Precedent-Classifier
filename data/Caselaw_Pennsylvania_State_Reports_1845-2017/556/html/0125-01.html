727 A.2d 124

In the Matter of Jay Logan TROUT.

No. 499 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

April 12, 1999.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 12th day of April, 1999, a Rule having been entered by this Court on March 4, 1999, pursuant to Rule 214(d)(1), Pa.R.D.E., directing Jay Logan Trout to show cause why he should not be placed on temporary suspension and no response thereto having been filed, it is hereby

ORDERED that the Rule is made absolute; Jay Logan Trout is placed on temporary suspension and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217; Pa.R.D.E.; and the matter is referred to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 214(f)(1) and (g), Pa.R.D.E.
