727 A.2d 122

Carmen BORGIA, Petitioner, v. PRUDENTIAL INSURANCE COMPANY, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

April 5, 1999.

*120 ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 5thday of April, 1999, the petition for allowance of appeal is GRANTED. The parties are directed to address the following issues:

1) Whether this matter is controlled by common law arbitration principles.
2) If so, whether the Superior Court exceeded its scope of review when it determined that this matter should not have been submitted to arbitration.
