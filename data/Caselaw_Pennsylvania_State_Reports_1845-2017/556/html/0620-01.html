730 A.2d 485

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent, v. Kurt FISCHER, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 15, 1999.

Michael M. Mustokoff, Jonathan L; Swichar, Philadelphia, for petitioner.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 15th day of June 1999, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, limited to the following issue:

Whether the Superior Court erred in affirming the trial court’s finding that trial counsel was not ineffective for failing to request that the trial court provide a specific jury instruction concerning petitioner’s “reasonable mistake as to consent.”
