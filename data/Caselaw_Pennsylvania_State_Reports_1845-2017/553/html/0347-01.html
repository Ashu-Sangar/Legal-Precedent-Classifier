COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent, v. George Bennett MELIUS, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Oct. 14, 1998.

*348ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 14th day of October, 1998, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED limited to the following issue:

Whether under the Pennsylvania Constitution, the requirements of probable cause and reasonable suspicion for warrantless stops pertain only to present or future criminal activity, and not past criminal activity.
