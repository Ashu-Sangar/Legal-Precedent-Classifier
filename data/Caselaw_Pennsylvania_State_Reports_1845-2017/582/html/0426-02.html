ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 15th day of April, 2005, the Joint Petition to Temporarily Suspend an Attorney is granted and, pursuant *427to Rule 214, Pa.R.D.E., Leighton Cohen is placed on temporary suspension and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.
