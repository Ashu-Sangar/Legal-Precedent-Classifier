PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 19th day of April, 2005, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board and Dissent to the Majority Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board dated January 24, 2005, it is hereby

It is further ORDERED that respondent shall pay costs to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.
