PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 26th day of September, 2005, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board dated July 8, 2005, Petitioner is reinstated to the practice of law. Pursuant to our Order of January 28, 2003, Petitioner is placed on probation for a period of two years, subject to the following:

1. Petitioner shall select a financial monitor, subject to the approval of the Office of Disciplinary Counsel.

*4512. The financial monitor shall be an attorney in good standing admitted to practice law in this Commonwealth.
