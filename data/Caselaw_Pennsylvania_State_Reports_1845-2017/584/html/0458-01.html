884 A.2d 847

In the Matter of Kristen K. TOLAND.

No. 1052 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Oct. 7, 2005.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 7th day of October, 2005, a Rule having been entered by this Court on August 10, 2005, pursuant to Rule 214(d)(1), Pa.R.D.E., directing Kristen K. Toland to show cause why she should not be placed on temporary suspension and no response thereto having been filed, it is hereby

ORDERED that the Rule is made absolute; Kristen K. Toland is placed on temporary suspension pursuant to Rule 214(d)(2), Pa.R.D.E., and she shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.; and the matter is referred to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 214(f)(1), Pa.R.D.E.
