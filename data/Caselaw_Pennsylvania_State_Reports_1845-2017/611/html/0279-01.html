ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 26th day of July 2011, upon the motion of Appellant’s counsel seeking dismissal of the present post-*280conviction appeal due to Appellant’s death, it is hereby ordered that the collateral appeal is DISMISSED.
