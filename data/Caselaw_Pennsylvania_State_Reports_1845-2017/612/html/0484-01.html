31 A.3d 656

Richard A. MARLETTE, Sr. and Marleen Marlette, His Wife v. STATE FARM MUTUAL AUTOMOBILE INSURANCE COMPANY and Herman L. Jordan. Petition of State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Nov. 7, 2011.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 7th day of November, 2011, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, LIMITED TO the issue set forth below. Allocatur is DENIED as to all remaining issues. The issue, as stated by Petitioner, is:

Did the Superior Court err (as identified in the dissenting opinion) in holding, in conflict with Allen v. M[e]llinger, that plaintiffs may recover delay damages based on the full amount of the jury verdict rather than on the legally recoverable molded verdict, which was reduced to reflect *485the insurance policy limits that plaintiffs were permitted to receive?
