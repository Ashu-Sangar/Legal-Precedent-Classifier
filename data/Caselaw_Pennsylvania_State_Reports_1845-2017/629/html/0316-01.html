105 A.3d 657

CLIPPER PIPE & SERVICE, INC. v. The OHIO CASUALTY INSURANCE CO.; Contracting Systems, Inc. II Petition of United States Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit.

No. 161 EM 2014.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Dec. 10, 2014.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 10th day of December, 2014, the Petition for Certification of Question of Law submitted by the United States Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit is GRANTED. This Court shall consider the following issue:

Under Pennsylvania law, does the Contractor and Subcontractor Payment Act (CASPA), 73 P.S. §§ 501-516, apply to a project where the owner is a governmental entity, such as the federal government in this case?

The Prothonotary is DIRECTED to establish a briefing schedule and list this matter for oral argument.
