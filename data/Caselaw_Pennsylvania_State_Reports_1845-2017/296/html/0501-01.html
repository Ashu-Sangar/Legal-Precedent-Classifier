Quaker City National Bank, Appellant, v. Brooks et al.

Argued April 15, 1929.

Before Moschzisker, C. J., Frazer, Walling, Simpson, Kephart, Sadler and Schaffer, JJ.

C. B. Wagoner, with him H. A. Gordon, J. W. McWilliams and Charles S. Wesley, for appellant.

Scott Seddon, for appellee.

May 13, 1929:

Per Curiam,

This is an appeal by plaintiff from an order discharging a rule for judgment against the garnishee on its answers to interrogatories. Such an order is interlocutory and not appealable: Brendlinger v. Riegel, 37 Pa. Superior Ct. 474.

The appeal is quashed.
