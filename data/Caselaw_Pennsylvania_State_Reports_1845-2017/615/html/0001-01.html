COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Petitioner v. Jack W. FORTENBAUGH, II, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 28, 2012.

ORDER

AND NOW, this 28th day of February, 2012, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, rephrased for clarity, is:

Did the Superior Court err in overturning the jury verdict and vacating the sentence because there were two inadvertent, unintentional references made to an offer to take a polygraph test?
