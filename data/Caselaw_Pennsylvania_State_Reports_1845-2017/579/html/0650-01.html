In the Matter of Andrew F. MALONE.

No. 953 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 1st day of September, 2004, the Joint Petition for Temporary Suspension is granted, Andrew F. The matter is hereby referred to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 214(f)(1), Pa.R.D.E.
