856 A.2d 33

In the Matter of Philip L. KANTOR

No. 908 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 29, 2004.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 29th day of June, 2004, Philip L. Kantor having been suspended from the practice of law in the State of New Jersey for a period of three months by Order of the Supreme Court of New Jersey dated November 21, 2003; the said Philip L. Kantor having been directed on March 30, 2004, to inform this Court of any claim he has that the imposition of the identical or comparable discipline in this Commonwealth would be unwarranted and the reasons therefor; and no response having been filed, it is

ORDERED that Philip L. Kantor is suspended from the practice of law in this Commonwealth for a period of three *388months, and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.
