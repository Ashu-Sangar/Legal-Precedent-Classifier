857 A.2d 1291

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Petitioner, v. Gregory H. KING, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 28, 2004.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 28th day of September, 2004, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED. The order of the Superior Court is VACATED, and this matter is REMANDED to that court for reconsideration in light of our decision in Commonwealth v. Young, 849 A.2d 1152 (Pa.2004). Jurisdiction relinquished.

Justice RUSSELL M. NIGRO notes his dissent to the per curiam order as he would deny the petition.
