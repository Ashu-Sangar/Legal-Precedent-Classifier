Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

The Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, and the order of the Commonwealth Court is REVERSED, pursuant to Alexander v. PennDOT, 583 Pa. 592, 880 A.2d 552 (2005), and McGrory v. PennDOT, 915 A.2d 1155 (Pa.2007). Jurisdiction relinquished.
