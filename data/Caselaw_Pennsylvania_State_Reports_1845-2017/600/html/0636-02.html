PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 15th day of April, 2009, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, LIMITED to the following two issues, as restated for clarity:

1. ) Whether the arbitrator’s findings of September 17, 2004 constituted a final award within the meaning of 42 Pa.C.S.A. § 7341?
2. ) Whether a trial court has equitable powers to terminate a common law arbitration prior to the entry of a final award by the arbitrator?
