797 A.2d 891

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Salvatore DeLELLO, Jr., Respondent.

No. 567 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

April 30, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 30th day of April, 2002, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board dated February 26, 2002, it is hereby

ORDERED that SALVATORE DeLELLO, JR., be and he is SUSPENDED from the Bar of this Commonwealth for a period of two (2) years, retroactive to March 3, 2000, the date of his temporary suspension, and he shall comply with all the provisions of Rule 217 Pa.R.D.E.

It is further ORDERED that respondent shall pay costs to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.
