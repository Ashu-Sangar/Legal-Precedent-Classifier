792 A.2d 590

In the Matter of Michael L. BRINT. Petition for reinstatement.

Nos. 346 Disciplinary Docket No. 1, 55 DB 1982.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 14, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 14th day of February, 2002, upon consideration of the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board of the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania dated December 19, 2001, the Petition for Reinstatement is granted.

Pursuant to Rule 218(e), Pa.R.D.E., petitioner is directed to pay the expenses incurred by the Board in the investigation and processing of the Petition for Reinstatement.
