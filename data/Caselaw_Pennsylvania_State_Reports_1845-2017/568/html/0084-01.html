792 A.2d 592

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Clark W. PEASE, Respondent.

Nos. 695 Disciplinary Docket No. 3, 143 DB 2001.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 27, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 27th day of February, 2002, on certification by the Disciplinary Board that the respondent, CLARK W. PEASE, who was suspended by Order of this Court dated October 11, 2001, for a period of three months, has filed a verified statement showing compliance with all the- terms and conditions of the Order of Suspension and Rule 217, Pa. R.D.E., and there being no other outstanding order of suspension or disbarment, CLARK W. PEASE, is hereby reinstated to active status, effective immediately.
