72 A.3d 263

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Petitioner v. Dennis BLAND, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

July 24, 2013.

*584 ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 24th day of July 2013, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, as stated by petitioner, is:

Did Superior Court err by suppressing a confession that respondent gave after receiving Miranda v. Arizona, 384 U.S. 436, 86 S.Ct. 1602, 16 L.Ed.2d 694 (1966), warnings because, six days prior to any police questioning, while in custody in another jurisdiction, he had signed a form antieipatorily declining to be interviewed?
