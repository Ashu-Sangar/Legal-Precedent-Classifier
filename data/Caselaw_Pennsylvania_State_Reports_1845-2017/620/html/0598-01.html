PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 11th day of July, 2013, the Petition for Alowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue is:

Did the Pennsylvania General Assembly intend to deprive families of children with autism of the right to appeal insurance denials to court[,] while granting that right to insurance companies?
