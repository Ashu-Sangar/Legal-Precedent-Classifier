OPINION

PER CURIAM.

In light of this Court’s recent decision in Unemployment Comp. Bd. of Rev. of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania et al. v. Sun Oil Co., 476 Pa. 589, 383 A.2d 519 (1978), the order of the Commonwealth Court dissolving the injunction in the instant case is affirmed, and the Board of Review’s determination of eligibility is affirmed.

PACKEL, J., did not participate in the consideration or decision of this case.
