PER CURIAM:

The Order of the Superior Court is reversed. 380 Pa.Super. 645, 547 A.2d 436 (1988). We conclude that the trial court did not abuse its discretion in excluding those portions of the victim’s diary which were protected by the Rape Shield Law, 18 Pa.C.S. § 3104. Commonwealth v. Majorana, 503 Pa. 602, 470 A.2d 80 (1983).
