Flowers Builders Supply Company, Appellant, v. Farrell Building Company.

Argued March 26, 1935.

Before Frazer, C. J., Simpson, Kephart, Schaerer, Maxey, Drew and Linn, JJ.

Virgil L. Johnson, with him J. A. Stranahan, of Stranahan & Sampson, for appellant.

Louis J. Wiesen and Fmrys G. Francis, for appellee, were not heard.

April 22, 1935:

Per Curiam,

An examination of the record in this case fails to disclose any reason for appointment of a receiver. The bill was properly dismissed.

Judgment affirmed at costs of appellant.
