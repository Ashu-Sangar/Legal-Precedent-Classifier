Jude BRUNSON, Petitioner v. PENNSYLVANIA DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS et al., Respondents.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 10, 2005.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 10th day of February, 2005, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal having been improvidently filéd, the document is hereby regarded as a notice of appeal from the order of the Commonwealth Court. See Pa.R.A.P. 1103. As Petitioner’s allegations of error are without merit, the order of the Commonwealth Court is hereby AFFIRMED.
