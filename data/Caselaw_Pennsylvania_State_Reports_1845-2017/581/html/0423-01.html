866 A.2d 253

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Robert Thomas GIBSON Respondent.

No. 777 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Nov. 23, 2004.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 23rd day of November, 2004, on certification by the Disciplinary Board that the respondent, ROBERT THOMAS GIBSON, who was suspended by Order of this Court dated November 4, 2004, for a period of one year retroactive to December 9, 2002, has filed a verified statement showing compliance with all the terms and conditions of the Order of Suspension and Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E., and there being *424no other outstanding order of suspension or disbarment, ROBERT THOMAS GIBSON is hereby reinstated to active status, effective immediately.
