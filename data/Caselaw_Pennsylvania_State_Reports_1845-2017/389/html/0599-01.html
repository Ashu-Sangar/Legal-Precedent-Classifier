Petrikin v. Heyburn, Appellant.

June 28, 1957:

Argued April 22, 1957.

Before Jones, C. J., Bell, Chidsey, Musmanno, Aenold and Jones, JJ.

reargument refused July 30, 1957.

Harry J. Rubin, Deputy Attorney General, with him, Thomas D. McBride, Attorney General, for appellants.

Sabato M. Bendiner, with him Mervin J. Hartman and Busser & Bendiner, for appellee.

Opinion by

Me. Justice Musmanno,

This is a companion ease to that of Smiley v. Heyburn, 389 Pa. 594, in which we today handed down an opinion reversing the order of the Court below. As the pleadings and issues raised in the instant case are identical with those raised in Smiley v. Heyburn, there is no need to repeat what we there said.

Order of the lower Court reversed.
