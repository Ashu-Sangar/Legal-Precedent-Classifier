Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, Appellant, v. G. E. Paul.

No. 105, July T., 1894. Appeal from Q. S. Phila. Co.

January 30, 1899:

Per Curiam,

The judgment of this Court heretofore entered (170 Pa. 296) having been reversed by the Supreme Court of the United States, it is now ordered that the judgment of the court of quarter sessions of Philadelphia county be and the same is hereby affirmed with costs.
