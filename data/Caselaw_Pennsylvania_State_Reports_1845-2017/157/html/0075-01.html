Opinion by

Mr. Justice Williams,

This case is fully ruled by the Appeal of W. F. Hallstead, guardian, etc., in which an opinion is this day filed. It is not necessary to repeat what is there said. It is enough to refer to the opinion in that case for the reasons in support of the decree in this. The assignments of error are not,, sustained and the decree of the court below is affirmed.
