PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 2nd day of January, 2001, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is granted, limited to the following issue:

Whether a party subject to a collective bargaining agreement that contains a grievance procedure can pursue a declaratory judgment action to litigate a work-related issue?
