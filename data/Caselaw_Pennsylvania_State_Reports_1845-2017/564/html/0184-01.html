765 A.2d 1115

J.H., Petitioner v. STATE ETHICS COMMISSION, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Jan. 19, 2001.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 19th day of January 2001, the petition for allowance of appeal is GRANTED, limited to the issue of whether one hired by a non-profit corporation to serve as chief executive officer of the Philadelphia Gas Works is a “public employee” subject to the state Ethics Act.
