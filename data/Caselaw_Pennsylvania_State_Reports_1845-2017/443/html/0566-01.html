R. M. Shoemaker Co., Appellant, v. Blumenfeld.

Argued April 21, 1971.

Before Jonhs, Eagen, O’Brien, Roberts, Pomeroy and Barbieri, JJ.

Victor Wright, with him Fox, Rothschild, O’Brien & Frankel, for appellant.

Milton S. Lamroff, with him Techner, Rubin and Shapiro, for appellees.

June 28, 1971 :

Opinion

Per Curiam,

This appeal being from an interlocutory order not made appealable by statute, the appeal is quashed. Appellant to pay costs.

Mr. Chief Justice Bell took no part in the consideration or decision of this case.
