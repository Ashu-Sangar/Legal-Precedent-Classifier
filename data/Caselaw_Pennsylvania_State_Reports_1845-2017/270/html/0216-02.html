Wartell, Appellant, v. Clearview Coal Co.

Appeal, No. 138, Jan. T., 1921.

April 11, 1921:

Per Curiam,

A stipulation has been filed of record in this case agreeing that the questions here involved are the same as those in Jordan v. Clearview Coal Company, the judgment in which was affirmed to-day; what is there said controls here.

Judgment affirmed.
