807 A.2d 830

Robin KRIPP, Respondent, v. Anthony KRIPP, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 23, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 23rd day of September, 2002, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED, LIMITED to the following issue:

Whether parol evidence should have been admitted to ascertain the parties’ intended definition of the term “cohabitation” in a property settlement agreement?

Justice EAKIN did not participate in the consideration of this matter.
