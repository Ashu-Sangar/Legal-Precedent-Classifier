Dunbar, to use, v. Mercer, Appellant.

Argued March 21, 1938.

Before Ejephart, C. J., Schaffer, Maxey, Drew, Linn, Stern and Barnes, JJ.

William A. Challener, with him William A. Challener, Jr., for appellant.

Burtt Harris, for appellee.

April 18, 1938:

Per Curiam,

The judgment of the Superior Court, reversing the judgment of the County Court of Allegheny County and granting a new trial, is affirmed on the opinion of Judge Parker. Costs to be paid by appellant.
