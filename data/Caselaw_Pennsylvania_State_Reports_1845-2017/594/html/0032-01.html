934 A.2d 1148

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent, v. Crystal Dawn WEIMER, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Oct. 1, 2007.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 1st day of October, 2007, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, LIMITED TO the issue set forth below. Allocatur is DENIED as to all remaining issues. The issue, rephrased for clarity, is:

Whether it is possible, as a matter of law, to be convicted of conspiracy to commit murder in the third degree?
