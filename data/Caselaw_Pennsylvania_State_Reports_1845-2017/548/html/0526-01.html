No. 355 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

July 15, 1997.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 15th day of July, 1997, an Order and Rule to Show Cause having been entered by this Court on June 20, 1997, and no response to the Rule to Show Cause having been filed, it is hereby ORDERED that:

1. The Rule is made absolute and, pursuant to Rule 208(f), Pa.R.D.E., respondent is placed on temporary suspension until further definitive action by this Court;

2. Respondent shall comply with the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E.;

3. The President Judge of the Court of Common Pleas of Allegheny County, in accordance with Rule 217(g), Pa.R.D.E., shall take such further action and make such further orders as may be necessary to fully protect the rights and interests of respondentâ€™s clients.

4. All financial institutions in which respondent holds accounts shall freeze such accounts pending further Order of this Court.

This Order constitutes an imposition of public discipline within the meaning of Rule 402, Pa.R.D.E., pertaining to confidentiality.
