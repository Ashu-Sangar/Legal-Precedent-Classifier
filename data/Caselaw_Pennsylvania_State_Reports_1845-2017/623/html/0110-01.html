Frances KEENE v. WORKERS’ COMPENSATION APPEAL BOARD (OGDEN CORPORATION). Petition of Ogden Corporation.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 9th day of December, 2013, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, the order of the Commonwealth Court is VACATED, and the matter is remanded for reconsideration in light of City of Pittsburgh and UPMC Benefit Management Services, Inc. v. Workers’ Compensation Appeal Board (Robinson), 620 Pa. 345, 67 A.3d 1194 (2013). The Petition for Supersedeas is DENIED.
