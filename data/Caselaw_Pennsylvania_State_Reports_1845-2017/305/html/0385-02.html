Lang et al., Appellants, v. Hanlon et al.

(No. 9,

March T., 1931.)

November 23, 1931:

Opinion by

Mr. Justice Maxey,

This is the appeal of William Lang and Elizabeth Lang from the judgment of the court below entering judgment n. o. v. in favor of the defendant, M. A. Hanlon. The judgment is affirmed for the reasons set forth in the opinion filed to No. 182, March Term, 1931.
