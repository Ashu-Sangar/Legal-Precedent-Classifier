Unterreiner et al., Appellants, v. Turtle Creek Borough.

Argued October 6, 1931.

Before Frazer, C. J., Walling, Simpson, Kephart, Schaefer, Maxey and Drew, JJ.

*347 Harry J. Nesbit, with him Michael E. Evashwick, for appellants.

G. Malcolm McDonald, for appellee.

November 23, 1931:

Per Curiam,

The order entering judgment non obstante veredicto for defendant is affirmed on the opinion of the learned trial judge which sets forth so fully and correctly the facts developed by the testimony and the law applicable thereto that nothing additional need be said by this court.

Judgment affirmed.
