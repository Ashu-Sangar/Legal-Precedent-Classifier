Willard MATTSON, Petitioner, v. WORKERS’ COMPENSATION APPEAL BOARD (ATACS CORPORATION), Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Dec. 14, 1998.

John J. Stanzione, Downingtown, for petitioner.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 14th day of December, 1998, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED, and the decision of the Commonwealth Court is REVERSED pursuant to Lykins v. Workers’ Compensation Appeal Board, 552 Pa. 1, 713 A.2d 77 (Pa.1998). The matter is REMANDED to the Workers’ Compensation Appeal Board for consideration of the merits of Petitioner’s penalty petition.
