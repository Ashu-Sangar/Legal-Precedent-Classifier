PER CURIAM.

Appellant alleges that he was denied effective assistance of counsel. The record fails to support this claim. See Commonwealth ex rel. Washington v. Maroney, 427 Pa. 599, 235 A.2d 349 (1974). See generally Commonwealth v. McGrogan, 449 Pa. 584, 297 A.2d 456 (1972).

Order affirmed.
