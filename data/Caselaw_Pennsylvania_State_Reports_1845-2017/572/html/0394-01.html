815 A.2d 628

NORTHWOOD CONSTRUCTION COMPANY, INC., Petitioner v. TOWNSHIP OF UPPER MORELAND, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Jan. 24, 2003.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 24th day of January 2003, we GRANT the Petition for Allowance of Appeal, LIMITED to the following issue:

Whether the Commonwealth Court erred in deciding that a municipality may impose a Business Privilege Tax on 100% of gross receipts of a business that is located in that subdivision but that performs a substantial amount of work outside of that subdivision?
