Sprague v. Zeck, Appellant, et al.

November 12, 1937:

Argued September 28, 1937.

Before Kephart, C. J., Schaefer, Maxey, Drew, Linn and Stern, JJ.

John H. Borg, for appellant.

John E. Evans, Jr., of Margiotti, Pugliese, Evans & Bucldey, for appellee.

Per Curiam,

We are of one mind that the evidence in this record demonstrates that the questions of negligence and contributory negligence were for the jury. Nothing would be gained by a detailed recital of the facts, as that is all the motion for judgment n. o. v. calls for.

Judgment affirmed.
