ORDER

PER CURIAM.

The Petition for Alowance of Appeal is hereby GRANTED and the Order of the Commonwealth Court is REVERSED. See McGrory v. Department of Transportation, Bureau of Driver Licensing, 591 Pa. 56, 915 A.2d 1155 (2007).
