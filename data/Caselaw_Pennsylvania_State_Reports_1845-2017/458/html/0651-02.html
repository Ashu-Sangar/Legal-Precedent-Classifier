Commonwealth v. Duffy, Appellant.

*652October 30, 1974:

Opinion

Per Curiam,

The petition for allocatur is granted. The order of the Superior Court and the judgment of sentence of the court of common pleas are reversed and a new trial is awarded. Commonwealth v. Contakos, 455 Pa. 136, 314 A.2d 259 (1974); Commonwealth v. Diana, 455 Pa. 267, 314 A.2d 262 (1974); Commonwealth v. Jackson, 457 Pa. 79, 319 A.2d 161 (1974). See Wardius v. Oregon, 412 U.S. 470 (1973).
