Opinion

Per Curiam,

Decree reversed and case remanded to the Pennsylvania Board of Probation and Parole for a counselled hearing on the revocation of parole. See Com. ex rel. Rambeau v. Board of Probation and Parole, 4 Pa. Commonwealth Ct. 152 (1972).

Mr. Chief Justice Jones, Mr. Justice Eagen, Mr. Justice Pomeroy and Mr. Justice Manderino adhere to the views that they expressed in Com. ex rel. Rambeau v. Board of Probation and Parole, 455 Pa. 8, 314 A. 2d 842 (1973).
