April 24, 1899:

Opinion by

Mb. Chief Justice Stebbett,

This and three other cases, involving substantially the same questions, were argued at last term with Commonwealth’s Appeal, Portuondo’s Estate, No. 440, January term, 1897, in which *67an opinion has just been filed holding the act of May 12,1887, unconstitutional, etc., ante, p. 28.

For reasons given in that opinion the decree in this case must be sustained.

Decree affirmed and appeal dismissed at appellant’s costs.
