108 A.3d 31

COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent, v. Karim Husein WRIGHT, Petitioner.

No. 182 MM 2014.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Jan. 23, 2015.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 23rd day of January, 2015, in response to the Petition for Leave to File Petition for Allowance of Appeal Nunc Pro Tunc, this matter is REMANDED to the common pleas court for appointment of new counsel within 30 days of this order. Newly-appointed counsel is DIRECTED to file a Petition for Allowance of Appeal within 60 days of appointment.

Justice Stevens notes his dissent and would deny the Petition for Leave to file a Petition for Allowance of Appeal Nunc Pro Tunc.
