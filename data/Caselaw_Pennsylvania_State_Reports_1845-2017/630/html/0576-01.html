PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 3rd day of December, 2014, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, as stated by Petitioner, is:

Whether the Superior Court of Pennsylvania erred in deciding under Pa.R.C.P. 2959(c) that Petitioner waived four of the six meritorious defenses put forth on appeal to open/ strike a confessed judgment, when those defenses were fleshed out in discovery and included in a court sanctioned supplemental brief?
