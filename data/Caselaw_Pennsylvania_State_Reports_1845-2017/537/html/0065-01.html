ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 5th day of April, 1994, the Joint Petition for Remand is granted. This matter is remanded to the Commonwealth Court for determination of the fees and costs to be awarded Appellee, Bloomingdale’s By Mail, Ltd. Jurisdiction is relinquished.
