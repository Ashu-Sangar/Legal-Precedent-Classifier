Susan E. Moyer, Asst. District Attorney, Joseph C. Madenspacher, District Attorney, for Com.

*114 ORDER

PER CURIAM.

Based upon our disposition in Commonwealth v. Williams, 557 Pa. 285, 733 A.2d 593 (1999), we affirm the Order of the Lancaster County Court of Common Pleas dated March 12, 1998, insofar as it is consistent with our disposition in Williams and we reverse its order insofar as it is inconsistent with the same. Furthermore, we remand the matter to the Superior Court for disposition of any remaining issues.

Jurisdiction is relinquished.
