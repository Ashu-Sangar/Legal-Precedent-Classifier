Mark R. Zimmer, Dist. Atty., Patrick L. Robinson, First Asst. D.A., for Commonwealth.

PER CURIAM.

Insofar as it is consistent with our dispositions in Commonwealth v. Williams, 557 Pa. 285, 733 A.2d 593 (1999) and Commonwealth v. Gaffney, 557 Pa. 327, 733 A.2d 616 (1999) we affirm the Order of the Wayne County Court of Common Pleas dated October 29, 1998. Furthermore, we remand the matter to the Superior Court for disposition of any remaining issues.

Jurisdiction is relinquished.
