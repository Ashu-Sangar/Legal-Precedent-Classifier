737 A.2d 738

Anna PENNACCHIO and Daniel Pennacchio, Respondents, v. Samuel M. SNIPES, Barbara T. Snipes, Bradshaw Snipes, and Ingeborg L. Snipes t/a Snipes Farm & Nursery, a Partnership, Petitioners.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 29, 1999.

Stephen N. Huntington, Philadelphia, for petitioner.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 29th day of September, 1999, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, and the order of the Superior Court is MODIFIED to provide as follows:

The order of the trial court granting summary judgment based upon the claim of immunity pursuant to the Recreation Use of Land and Water Act, 68 P.S. §§ 477-1, et seq., is REVERSED, and the matter remanded to the trial court *409for consideration of the additional ground for relief asserted in Defendants’ motion for summary judgment, namely, the claim to immunity pursuant to the Agricultural Immunity Act, 42 Pa.C.S. § 8339.

Jurisdiction is relinquished.
