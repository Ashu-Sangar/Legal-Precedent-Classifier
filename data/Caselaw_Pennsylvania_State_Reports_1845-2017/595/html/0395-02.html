PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 12th day of DECEMBER, 2007, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The issue, rephrased for clarity, is:

Whether the Commonwealth can prevail in a common law forfeiture matter where it did not offer evidence that the items Petitioner sought to have returned were linked to the crimes to which he pled guilty?
