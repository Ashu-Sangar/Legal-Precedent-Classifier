PER CURIAM.

In light of Commonwealth v. White, 515 Pa. 348, 528 A.2d 596 (1987), and Commonwealth v. Dunbar, 503 Pa. 590, 470 A.2d 74 (1983), the Order of the Superior Court, 409 Pa.Super. 656, 589 A.2d 1176, remanding the case to the Court of Common Pleas is reversed and the conviction is affirmed.
