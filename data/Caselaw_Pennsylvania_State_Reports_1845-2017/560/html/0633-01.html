The BIRTH CENTER v. The ST. PAUL COMPANIES, INC., Sharon Dawson-Coates and Al Afonso. Petition of St. Paul Companies, Inc.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

March 1, 2000.

*634Thomas P. Wagner, Joshua Bachraeh, Carl D. Buchholz, Philadelphia, for petitioner.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 1st day of March, 2000, we GRANT the Petitions for Allowance of Appeal and we LIMIT the appeals to the following issues:

a. Whether the insurer’s payment of the excess verdict precludes insured from maintaining a bad faith action;
b. Whether the trial court properly charged the jury on a breach of contract theory based on the insurer’s bad faith conduct; and,
c. Whether compensatory damages are recoverable in a bad faith action.
