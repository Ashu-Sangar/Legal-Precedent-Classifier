651 A.2d 135

OFFICE OF DISCIPLINARY COUNSEL, Petitioner, v. Everett P. PRIESTLEY, Respondent.

No. 55 Disciplinary Docket No. 3.

Disciplinary Board No. 116 DB 94.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Nov. 1, 1994.

ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 1st day of November, 1994, there having been filed with this Court by Everett P. Priestley his verified Statement of Resignation dated September 20, 1994, stating that he desires to resign from the Bar of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania in accordance with the provisions of Rule 215, Pa.R.D.E., it is

ORDERED that the resignation of Everett P. Priestley be and it is hereby accepted and he is DISBARRED ON CONSENT from the Bar of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania; and it is further ORDERED that he shall comply with the provisions of Rule 217, Pa.R.D.E. Respondent shall pay costs, if any, to the Disciplinary Board pursuant to Rule 208(g), Pa.R.D.E.

MONTEMURO, J., is sitting by designation.
