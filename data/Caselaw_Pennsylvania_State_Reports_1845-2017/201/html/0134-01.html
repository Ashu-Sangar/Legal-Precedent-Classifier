Boucher, Admr., Appellant, v. Pennsylvania Railroad.

Argued Oct. 14, 1901.

Appeal, No. 70, Oct. T., 1901,' by plaintiff, from order of C. P. Westmoreland Co., Aug. T., 1898, No. 643, refusing to take off nonsuit in case of John N. Boucher, Administrator of Hamilton Smith, v. Pennsylvania Railroad Company.

Before McCollum, C. J., Mitchell, Dean, Fell, Bbown, Mestbezat and Pottee, JJ.

Affirmed.

*135January 6, 1902:

Opinion by

Mb. Justice Fell,

This appeal raises the same question that has been considered in the case of Smith v. Penna. R. R. Co., and for the reasons stated in the opinion filed in that ease the judgment is affirmed.
